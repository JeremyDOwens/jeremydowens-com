About This Site

02 May 2019

After doing some self-evaluation, I realized that despite putting in loads of effort, it can be hard for me to describe my skills and achievements. I haven't done the best job recording my progress, and I don't have a whole lot of experience to reference. In my current job, I spend much of my time developing a web application, so I decided to start a personal project where I can highlight the skills that I have learned on the job while chronicling my ongoing learning experiences. The first project I work on will be fleshing out this web application, turning it from the basic shell that it is now into a usable blogging site. Throughout that time, I will be developing other skills on my own, and for my classes at Syracuse. I hope to paint a good picture of my professional development, and maybe even help some others learn along the way.
